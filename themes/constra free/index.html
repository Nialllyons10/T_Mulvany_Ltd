We Are Specialists In

What We Do

New Build Homes

We have a history of constructing a wide range of residential properties both traditional and modern style builds.

Home Extensions

We have vast experience in home extensions and making them fit in with the existing building. The home extensions give our clients the extra living space that they desire.

Churches

We have a huge range of experience in restoration and renovation of churches. We specialise in the heritage and restoration of old churches. We have carried out a wide range of projects including internal and external work. Externally, the work ranges from roofing to restoring old stonework with traditional pointing. Internally, restoring plaster work with traditional materials, Sanctuary restoration or modernisation (Alters etc.)

Schools

We have a range of experience with school construction. These projects include single storey extensions, two storey extensions and construction of sports halls.

Industrial Units

We have carried out several new build industrial units over the years for a wide range of clients. These units ranged in all size up to 20000sq feet

Renovations and Restoration

We specialise in heritage and restoration work. Work includes traditional style pointing in old stonework, restoration of internal plaster, and re-roofing of existing run down buildings.
